About Us

Company Address: Yuncheng City, Shanxi Province, the hometown of Guan Yu and the land of the phoenix

Year of Establishment: 2013
Registered Capital: 50 million yuan
Coverage Area: Over 100 acres

Since its establishment, Shanxi Dawei Clothing Co., Ltd. has consistently adhered to the business philosophy of “promoting brands with integrity, winning trust with quality, managing scientifically and normatively, and gathering excellent talents,” achieving significant development. The company’s annual production capacity reaches 5 million sets, with clients spanning across more than 20 provinces, cities, and autonomous regions nationwide, including Shanxi, Gansu, Ningxia, and Xinjiang. We have successfully established our own brand “Shengdawei.”

Equipment and Technology

Dawei Clothing has introduced advanced production equipment from various countries around the world, including:

  • France: CAD design and pattern-making systems and CAM automatic cutting beds from Lectra
  • Germany: Automatic hanging lines and hem finishing machines from Dürkopp Adler
  • Italy: Pressing equipment from Macpi
  • Japan: Sewing equipment from Juki

We have established standardized design, production, sales, and service systems, accumulating rich experience particularly in serving the court, market supervision, and urban management industry clothing sectors. The company was among the first in the industry to pass ISO9001 Quality Management System certification, ISO14001 Environmental Management System certification, OHSAS18001 Occupational Health and Safety Management System certification, and has received honors such as “AAA-level Quality Integrity Enterprise.”

“Eliminate carelessness, enhance product quality”
This is our production philosophy. Products go through over 200 processes from fabric preshrinking, cutting, sewing, pressing to warehouse sales, completed by nearly 100 people working together.

Production and Quality Control

To ensure high product quality, we use Italian Bella’s fully automatic preshrinking machines, which increase fabric elasticity and prevent deformation through high temperature and strong vibration. The key to garment shaping lies in our use of German Dürkopp Adler’s hem finishing machines, making hems straight as a line and thin as a knife. The sewing base mainly uses Japanese Juki and German Dürkopp Adler equipment, with employees undergoing strict training before starting work. We have also introduced German fully automatic hanging lines and Italian Macpi’s pressing equipment to ensure perfect garment shaping.

After-sales Service

We adhere to the after-sales service principle of “creating satisfactory windows, striving for first-class service,” with a rapid-response after-sales service system including work processes, computer information databases, and corresponding hardware and personnel support, ready to solve problems for customers at any time.

Dawei Clothing will demonstrate the spirit of Dawei Clothing and promote the spirit of Shanxi merchants with sincere cooperation and first-class service quality, creating a brilliant future.
